Our Story

Bellpa General Store has been serving the community since 2025. Founded by the Isabell and Paul family, our mission has always been to provide fresh, affordable goods with a smile.

We believe a neighborhood store should be more than just a place to shop; it should be a place where everyone feels welcome.
